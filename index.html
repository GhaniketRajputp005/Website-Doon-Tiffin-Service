About DTS(Who We Are🤔)

Established in 2012, our Student Mess is more than just a place to eat. it's a vibrant community hub. Founded with the vision of fostering connections and providing a home away from home, our Mess has been a cornerstone of student life.

We believe in the power of shared meals to create lasting friendships and enhance the overall student experience. From delicious and nutritious meals to a warm and welcoming environment, our Mess is where students come together to create memories and navigate university life as a united community.

    🟢 We proudly offer only vegetarian meals.
